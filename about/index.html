About

A site centered on the work.

This archive exists to publish and support papers, articles, charts, and related materials in a format that is clear, durable, and easy to revisit.

The purpose of this site is simple: to give the underlying work a stable public home. It begins with The Van Vliet Bitcoin Price Conjecture and will expand, over time, to include supporting articles, figures, tables, and other materials that clarify or test the thesis.

The emphasis is not on biography or constant output, but on publication-quality writing and supporting research that can be read carefully and returned to later.

Future work may widen into additional theories, essays, and related lines of inquiry. For now, the central aim is to keep attention on the paper and the ideas that support it.
