About

A short note on the site and its purpose.

This website is intended as a personal archive for serious writing, published under my own name and oriented toward durable public reading.

The site serves as a home for papers, conjectures, and long-form essays, beginning with work related to Bitcoin, monetary systems, and economic thought.

The emphasis is on clarity, restraint, and publication-quality presentation. Rather than constant output, the aim is to gather work that can be revisited, cited, and developed over time.

This page is currently a placeholder and will later be expanded with a fuller biographical note and broader context for the research collected here.
