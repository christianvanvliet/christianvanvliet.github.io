Featured Research

The Van Vliet Bitcoin Price Conjecture

A production-anchor framework exploring Bitcoin price formation through mining economics and monetary demand.

This paper advances a conjectural framework for understanding Bitcoin’s long-run price behavior through the interaction of production cost, issuance, miner economics, and monetary demand. It is presented as a research paper for public reading, critique, and refinement.

The current edition is intended as the first formal publication in a broader body of work concerning Bitcoin valuation, market structure, and economic narrative.

About

A site centered on the work.

This archive exists to publish and support papers, articles, charts, and related materials in a format that is clear, durable, and easy to revisit.

The site begins with The Van Vliet Bitcoin Price Conjecture and will expand, over time, to include supporting articles, figures, tables, and other materials that clarify or test the thesis.

The emphasis is not on biography or constant output, but on publication-quality writing and supporting research that can be read carefully and returned to later.
